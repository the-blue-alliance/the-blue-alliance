Brand Guidelines

Thanks for your interest our branding. We have a few guidelines for using our brand resources. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with them.
You can find downloadable assets in our GitHub.

Our Colors

Blue is our primary color. We use Yellow for accents.

Hex: #3F51B5 RGB: 63 / 81 / 181 CMYK: 65 / 55 / 0 / 29

Accent

Our Logo

The logo can only appear in blue, white or black.
You are free to use these logos when referring to or linking to The Blue Alliance.
Please do not make derivatives of these logos or use them to brand unofficial The Blue Alliance products so we can maintain our visual identity.

Please don’t do these things

Don't rotate the logo.
Don't change the logo’s proportion (e.g. skew, stretch or rotate).
Don't change the elements.
Don't re-color the colors.
Don't add drop shadows or any other effects.
Don't put on distracting backgrounds or colors.

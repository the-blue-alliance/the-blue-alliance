Webcast Formatting Protips

Livestream

URLs are like http://new.livestream.com/nefirst/BeantownBlitz2014. Inspect the HTML, find the embed widget, look for account=7031031&event=3138794. The "Channel" is the account ID, and the "File" is the event ID.

Ustream

URLs are like http://www.ustream.tv/channel/nasa-media-channel. Click "Embed," look for http://www.ustream.tv/embed/10414700. The "Channel" in this case is 10414700.
